About Us

About IITA

The International Institute of Tropical Agriculture (IITA) empowers students and professionals by supporting agriculture and research innovations for Africa's development.

Our Mission

To enhance food security, improve livelihoods, and promote sustainable agriculture in tropical Africa through training, partnerships, and innovation.

Programs

  • Industrial Training (SIWES)
  • Graduate Research Opportunities
  • Agricultural Field Internships

Why Choose IITA?

IITA is recognized globally for its research excellence, modern facilities, mentorship, and opportunities to work on real-world agricultural problems.
